About Project: Akihabara
In this pandemic-stricken land, entertainment has been experiencing a massive influx of viewers over the past few months. With this in mind, we integrate the spirit of community by assisting those who don't know where to start by recommending Japanese films that we, as viewers, enjoy. With the opinions of several hundred viewers we proudly present "Project: Akihabara", your one-stop drop to the world of Anime and Manga.
