The Road Back

The online learning made me realize how much I took face to face learning for granted. Although I say I'm stronger mentally now, I can't help but reminisce on the times before. It helps me relish in the moments we've had as friends and as classmates. Relishing im my old memories helps look forward to a future with my said friends. A future where we can hang around like the old days. I miss those days, but times have changed now. I no longer wish to return to the past, but I hold my head high for a brighter future.
