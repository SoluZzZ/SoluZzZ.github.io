The Return

It is Grade 10 now, there is some hope for the end of the pandemic. I am staying strong and steadfast in my studies. I'd like to say that I have adjusted to the online learning. I expect there to be hardships, however I believe I can get through this. This is my new world now. This is my last year and this is my time to finally conquer this path I had taken.

Struggle, endure, contend. For that alone is the sword of one who defies death. - Berserk, by Kentaro Miura
